About Us

Skillsforce is a skills search tool designed to increase the inclusivity of internal staffing processes, help organisations eliminate bias, and build diverse teams.

  • Candidates can be sure that only their relevant skills are being considered, and selection is merit-based. The process doesn't favour natural self promoters.
  • Hiring managers can ensure they are building diverse teams by choosing team members with different skill sets, and that their unconscious biases aren't causing unfairness and are not impacting project success.
